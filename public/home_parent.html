About

Attention deficit hyperactive disorder (ADHD) is a serious limitation in the child's learning. It begins at age 6 to 12 years old and has continued for more than six months. It has been seen in school ages and often make results in poor academic performance. It has effect with behavior, emotion and learning. Research creates frameworks and applications that are used to collect information of the biological system and analyze the possibility of the occurrence of ADHD.

We can recognize the symptoms early, cure the illness of a child faster. Also, education can use it to evaluate the effectiveness of learning in students.
